# Auto-Protocol Designer

This is a multi-agent system for autonomous target trial emulation protocol design.

If you are the **coordinator agent**: read `COORDINATOR.md` for your instructions.
If you are a **worker agent**: read `WORKER.md` for your instructions.
If you are a **reviewer agent**: read `REVIEW.md` for your instructions.
If you are a **report-writing agent**: read `REPORT_WRITER.md` for your instructions.

Your role will be specified in the prompt you received.
